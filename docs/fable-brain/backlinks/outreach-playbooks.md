# Outreach Playbooks

Per-opportunity-type: how to find them, qualify, and what the play is. Drafts come
from `outreach-templates.md`; the owner sends. Policy always applies.

## Smart Garage Doors

**Local business partnership (realtors, locksmiths, gate/fence cos, electricians, builders)**
- Find: `{trade} + {city}` searches; owner's real-world contacts FIRST (warmest).
- Play: genuine mutual-referral relationship; the link ("trusted partners" /
  "recommended by") is the byproduct. Offer to refer both ways — we get "is my
  garage door dead?" calls that are really gate/electrical issues, and vice versa.
- Qualify: real local business, active site, actually adjacent trade.

**Supplier / manufacturer / dealer pages**
- Play: if a dealer/authorized relationship exists (owner must confirm which),
  request locator inclusion via the manufacturer's dealer program. Also: a genuine
  testimonial about a supplier's product for their testimonial page (link back is
  customary — never demanded).

**Chamber / association (chambers, IDA, BOMA/IREM)**
- Play: paid memberships with directory listings + events. Value = the ROOM
  (property managers at BOMA events) more than the link. Owner approves spend.

**Property-manager / condo / co-op / HOA vendor resources**
- Find: `"approved vendors" OR "vendor list" property management {county}`;
  management-company websites with vendor pages; board-resource sites.
- Play: offer the genuinely useful asset (parking-garage-door maintenance guide,
  vendor-vetting checklist — both exist as blog guides) + COI-ready vendor intro.
  This is the highest business-value lane SGD has: link + recurring account.

**Local news / PR** → see `digital-pr-plan.md`. **Expert quotes**: monitor
Connectively/Help-a-B2B-Writer weekly; answer only queries where we have real
expertise; 3–5 sentence punchy answers; owner's name.

**Broken link replacement**
- Find: local resource pages linking to defunct garage door companies (search
  competitors that closed + `link:` style research once tool data exists).
- Play: "that company appears closed — we're a local alternative, here's our
  equivalent resource." Only where our replacement is genuinely equivalent.

**Unlinked brand mentions**
- Find: `"smart garage doors" -site:smartestgaragedoors.com` (+ social-free
  variants) monthly. Play: thank them, ask for the link. Near-free wins.

**Testimonial-for-link**: for suppliers/tools the business genuinely uses (parts
suppliers, software). Honest quotes only.

**Sponsorship / community**: local youth sports, street fairs, school events in
service areas. Sponsor pages usually link. Value = local goodwill first. Owner
approves spend; `rel=sponsored` expected.

## Zack & Elle

**Jewelry blog / editorial placement**
- The roundup lane (Knot/Good Trade/Forbes/Zoe Report — CSV has URLs): pitch =
  what makes us DIFFERENT (two-generation Diamond District family + IGI-certified
  catalog + accessible pricing), offer product loans/samples for testing, press
  images ready. **Prerequisite: review platform live** — editors check.
- Retailer-review sites (LabGrownCarats, LabDiamondsReviews, Ringspo): invite
  them to review us; provide access, answer everything honestly.

**Gift guide / product feature**
- Find: last year's holiday gift guides mentioning jewelry (search `"gift guide"
  jewelry 2026 {outlet-class}`); editors work Aug–Oct for holiday.
- Play: 2–3 hero products with prices, hi-res images, one-line why-it's-giftable.

**Wedding/engagement vendor partnership**
- Planners, photographers, venues in NYC: cross-referral + their "preferred
  vendors" pages. Same mutual-value logic as SGD partnerships.
- Directories (Knot/WeddingWire storefronts) = paid, owner decision, `sponsored`.

**Influencer/blogger collaboration**: gifting/loans with REQUIRED FTC disclosure;
track in CSV as sponsored; value = audience first, link second. No pay-for-
followed-links.

## Trojan

**Podcast/interview** (GATED on pen-name audio policy): find via podcast search
("influence", "persuasion", "dark psychology", indie-author shows); pitch = 3
specific segment ideas in the book's voice, not "I wrote a book."
**Book review circuit**: Reedsy Discovery/IndieReader/Kirkus Indie (paid ones

**Guest essays**: individually written pieces on influence psychology for
newsletters/blogs in the self-improvement space; bio link to trojanbook.com.
**Newsletter cross-promo**: chapter-1 magnet swaps with adjacent authors.

## Universal qualification gate (all playbooks)

Before any row goes to outreach: real site with a real audience? topical or
geographic fit? would this link make sense if Google didn't exist? contact is a
person, not info@spam? If any answer is no → `rejected`.
