## 1. Smart Garage Doors — smartestgaragedoors.com

- **Model:** local garage door repair/installation, NY/NJ/CT tri-state; premium-local
  positioning; owner priority = commercial (warehouses, docks, property managers).
- **Tech:** Vite/React + Puppeteer prerender, Vercel. Site brain:
  `docs/fable-website-brain/` — its rules govern all on-site work.
- **Priority link targets (deep pages, not just homepage):**
  `/commercial-garage-door-repair/`, `/loading-dock-door-repair/`,
  `/property-managers/`, `/commercial-maintenance-contracts/`, top city pages
  (`/queens-ny/`, `/brooklyn-ny/`, `/new-rochelle-ny/`, `/white-plains-ny/`,
  `/flushing-ny/`), cost guides, `/blog/*` guides (already link-worthy).
- **Do NOT promote:** `/report/`, `/lp/*`, thank-you pages (noindexed utility).
- **Keywords:** garage door repair {city}; commercial garage door repair; loading
  dock door repair; garage door spring replacement; + cost/comparison long-tails.
- **Conversion goals:** calls, WhatsApp, booking form, CommercialLeadForm
  (vendor-account requests) — all tracked.
- **Voice:** plain, honest, local tradesman-professional. No hype, no arrival-time
  promises (LSA), no hub-city disclosure, "complimentary estimate" phrasing.
- **Backlink strategy:** local citations + trade directories (ThomasNet, IDA) +
  chambers + property-manager/vendor resource pages + supplier/dealer locators +
  local PR + real-partner referral links (realtors, locksmiths, gate/fence
  contractors, electricians). Commercial relevance beats raw DA every time.
