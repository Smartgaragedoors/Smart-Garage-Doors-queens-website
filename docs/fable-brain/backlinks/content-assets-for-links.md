## Rules

1. An asset ships only if it clears the site's own brain rules (SGD:
   `docs/fable-website-brain/` — claims, prerender, sitemap wiring).
2. Assets marked **blocked on owner** go in the ask-list — models must not fake
   photos, founder anecdotes, or review counts to unblock themselves.
3. Every shipped asset gets: internal links from related pages, an entry in the
   CSV pipeline for who to show it to, and a line in the decision log.
