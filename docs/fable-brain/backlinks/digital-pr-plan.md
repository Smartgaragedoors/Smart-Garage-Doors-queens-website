# Digital PR Plan

PR here means: real stories real outlets would run, that happen to build authority.
No fabricated surveys, no fake "studies." Every angle below uses assets the
businesses actually have. Pitches are drafted by AI, sent by the owner.

## Smart Garage Doors

**Outlets worth building a list for (research each before pitching):** Queens
Chronicle, QNS.com, Brooklyn Eagle, Patch (Queens/Westchester/New Rochelle/White
Plains editions), Westchester Business Journal, lohud, local TV consumer/home
segments, hyperlocal newsletters. Trade: garage-door/door-industry publications
(IDA's publication, Door + Access Systems).

| Angle | Story | Peg / when |
|---|---|---|
| Home safety | "The garage door is the heaviest moving object in your home" — spring dangers, kid/pet safety, what never to DIY | Evergreen; spikes after any local garage-injury news |
| Storm/winter prep | Pre-winter garage door checklist for NY homes (real technician advice) | Oct–Nov; before named storms |
| Emergency prevention | The 5 warning signs before a door fails (real service-call data patterns) | Evergreen |
| Small-business story | Local family business built on answering the phone 24/7; the anti-call-center angle | Local-business features, holidays |
| Commercial/property ops | What a stuck parking-garage gate does to a 200-unit building; maintenance-contract economics | Property-management trade press, BOMA/IREM newsletters |
| Real jobs | Before/after of an unusual rescue (car trapped, brownstone retrofit, dock-door rebuild) — with photos | As they happen; needs owner photo flow |
| Expert quotes | Owner as the garage-door source for reporter queries (HARO/Connectively, Help a B2B Writer, local reporter relationships) | Ongoing — cheapest real links in the niche |

**Constraints:** LSA language rules apply in press too (no arrival-time promises).
The anchor logistics client stays anonymous. No staged "incidents."

## Cross-cutting PR rules

- One outlet, one personalized pitch; never the same draft to competing outlets
  simultaneously without noting non-exclusivity.
- Every published win: log in CSV, thank the writer, offer future expert access —
  the second link from a relationship is nearly free.
- Reporter deadlines beat our schedule; respond same-day to any journalist reply.
